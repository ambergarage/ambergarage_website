About the studio - Amber Garage

Amber Garage is a Silicon Valley based creative art & tech studio, founded by Botao (Amber) Hu. We utilize robotics technology for creating new media and new media art. As artists, we're creating arts based on our own media and tool to bridge the gap between virtual world and reality. As roboticists, we're thinking how robotics technology can revolutionize the process of creative works.
